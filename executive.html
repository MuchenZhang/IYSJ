Jonathan Liu

Founder

As of June 2020, Jonathan has graduated from Stamford American International School, Singapore, and will be attending UC Berkeley in the fall. He started the International Youth Science Journal in August 2018 as a way to share his enthusiasm for science with the online youth community. Very passionate about Biochemistry and really anything STEM-related, he founded and led his high school’s Science Society, and conducted research with NanoBio Lab under A*STAR over the summer. Being a keen Maker, he’s DIYed everything from a quadcopter to a 1:1 replica of a Portal Gun from Portal. Some of Jonathan’s other hobbies are playing board games, watching movies, and playing percussion. He hopes that IYSJ will live on after his time here and welcomes any suggestions, questions, concerns, comments, or criticisms to be sent to iysjournal@gmail.com or that.jonathan.liu@berkeley.edu
