Become a Guest Writer

If you would like to be a guest writer for the International Youth Science Journal and would like to publish an individual piece, please check if your article follows the guidelines outlined below. To submit your article, fill in the form here. You will be notified via email if we decide to publish your work, and you will receive full credit. Please note that once the International Youth Science Journal has decided to publish your work, you may not submit it for publication to any other platform.


Article Guidelines

Word count:

Preferably 600~1000(Minimum 500, maximum 1300)

*if you write over the word count, we might have to remove some sections of your article. *This guideline for word count doesn't apply to research papers.


Content:

Introduce and explain a scientific concept/theory/phenomenon in the following categories:

  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Physics
  • Computer Science
  • Engineering
  • Technology
  • Math
  • May integrate with other disciplines

    • Remember, you are writing for students, not professors so please do not assume high-level of prior knowledge. Explain terminology and use analogies for complex ideas.


    Citations:

    Please include a bibliography/works cited section at the end of your article. All citations must be of the latest APA format. Here is a useful online citation maker. In-text citations are required. We will not accept work submitted without proper citations.


    Special categories:

  • News (example): A recent event (past month) analysis from a science point of view.
  • Interview(example): Must be from primary source.
  • Research Peper(example): Follow the scientific method and have the method/data on a separate document.
