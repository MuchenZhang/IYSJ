For Students

We aim to provide secondary students with bite-sized science content that can hopefully inspire them to further explore their interests in the STEM field. All of our writers and editors are high schoolers, and we aim to provide digestible articles that make science both more interesting and more accessible.


For Educators

Here at IYSJ, we believe in the importance of scientific communication and that making knowledge understandable to everyone is arguably as important as generating the knowledge themselves. Our goal is to promote science literacy so feel free to use/reuse/adapt any of our articles for any educational purposes as long as you give credit. All articles in the International Youth Science Journal(IYSJ) are fact-checked and well-cited. Through our policies on article sources, we hope to become a high-quality source of science literature for secondary students.
